## Finally, a multi-miner that allows you to mine three coins at once!

Tired of choosing between your favorite cryptocurrencies? With **MultiMiner Pro**, that decision is a thing of the past. We have developed a revolutionary mining solution that allows you to fully leverage your computer's processing power, simultaneously mining **Monero (XMR)**, **Ravencoin (RVN)**, and **Litecoin (LTC)**.

### ✨ Featured Characteristics:

-   **Triple Simultaneous Mining**: Forget about switching miners. MultiMiner Pro manages the mining of Monero, Ravencoin, and Litecoin concurrently.
-   **Intelligent Resource Optimization**: Our advanced algorithm detects the available power of your CPU and GPU, dynamically allocating resources to maximize efficiency at all times. Your hardware will always be working on the most profitable tasks!
-   **Configurable Mining Priority**: The miner prioritizes **Ravencoin (KawPow)** for **low-power** machines, **Monero (RandomX)** for **intermediate power**, and **Litecoin (Scrypt)** for **high-power** machines, ensuring that each resource is used optimally.
-   **Easy to Use**: Simple configuration with a single `config.json` file. Download, set up your wallets, and start mining in minutes!
-   **Superior Performance**: Based on the robust XMRig architecture, optimized to offer the best possible hashrate for each algorithm.

## 💡 How It Works?

MultiMiner Pro constantly evaluates your equipment's performance and the demand for each algorithm. It uses your CPU for Monero (RandomX), your GPU for Ravencoin (KawPow), and the remaining CPU resources for Litecoin (Scrypt), intelligently balancing the load for unprecedented efficiency.

## ⬇️ Download and Installation

### 🪟 For Windows Users (Easy GUI)
1.  **Download the Package**: [**Download MultiMiner Pro v1.2.0 (Windows/Linux)**](https://github.com/luzbell-glitch/MultiMiner-Pro/releases/latest/download/MultiMiner-Pro-v1.2.0.zip)
2.  **Unzip** the ZIP file.
3.  **Run the Interface**: Right-click on the `run_windows.ps1` file and select **"Run with PowerShell"**.
4.  **Enter Your Wallets**: A window will open with three boxes. Paste your Monero, Ravencoin, and Litecoin addresses.
5.  **Click Start!**: Click the green button and the miner will start working automatically.

### 🐧 For Linux Users (Terminal)
1.  **Download and Unzip**:
    ```bash
    unzip MultiMiner-Pro-v1.2.0.zip
    cd MultiMiner-Pro
    ```
2.  **Start Mining**: Run the script by passing your three wallets:
    ```bash
    chmod +x run.sh
    ./run.sh <XMR_WALLET> <RVN_WALLET> <LTC_WALLET>
    ```

And that's it! MultiMiner Pro will start working, optimizing your Monero, Ravencoin, and Litecoin mining.
